About Us

Café Story

Coffee is believed to have originated in the Arab world, specifically in Ethiopia. The first credible historical evidence of coffee drinking appears in the Sufi monasteries of Yemen in the 15th century. Coffee was consumed for its stimulating properties and soon spread to the rest of the Arabian Peninsula.
